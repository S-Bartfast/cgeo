Tap coordinate buttons to toggle between digits and letters

Long-press on coordinate buttons to set custom a digit or letter (or space to hide)

The available operators for use in expressions are:
'+':  +42  and  40 + 2 
'-':  -42  and  40 - 2 
'*': multiplication,  6*9 
'/': division,  a/b 
'^': power notation,  2^4 
'%': remainder after division,  54 % 13 
'(' and ')': priority,  (1+2)/(a+b)
